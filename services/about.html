Our Story

Bogati Tech was founded in 2024 with a vision to transform the digital landscape in Nepal. What started as a small team of passionate tech enthusiasts has now grown into a full-service IT company serving clients across the globe.

Our journey began when our founder, Narayan Bogatgi, recognized the need for innovative digital solutions in the local market. With a background in computer science and a passion for technology, he assembled a team of like-minded professionals to create impactful digital experiences.

Today, we are proud to be one of the leading IT service providers in Nepal, offering a comprehensive range of services including Web Development, App Development, Digital Marketing, Photography & Videography, Business Consulting, and Graphic Designing.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Guiding principles that drive our company forward

Our Mission

To empower businesses with innovative digital solutions that drive growth, enhance efficiency, and create meaningful connections with their audience. We are committed to delivering excellence in every project and exceeding client expectations.

Our Vision

To be the leading IT service provider in Nepal and a globally recognized name in digital innovation. We aspire to create a digital ecosystem where businesses of all sizes can thrive and reach their full potential through technology.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Excellence, Collaboration, and Client-Centricity form the core of our values. We believe in transparent communication, creative problem-solving, continuous improvement, teamwork, and putting our clients' needs first.
